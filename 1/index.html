(Part 2) Architectural Perspective Compression

The images of the street do not focus on a single building but rather the overall street scene. Specifically, the nearest tree in both images is the same tree, and the side of the building on the right pictures the same part of that building. The zoomed-in image compresses the scene such that the distance between the trees seems to be much closer than it is in the image without any zoom. Similarly, the side of the building is extremely distorted and squished together in the zoomed-in image.
